# Product Overview

## One-line pitch

News Summariser turns noisy daily headlines into actionable PM-grade briefings with sentiment and cost visibility.

## Problem statement

Professionals face information overload from fragmented news feeds and limited time for synthesis.

## User value

- Saves reading time with concise summaries
- Surfaces narrative direction with sentiment analysis
- Improves trust through run-level observability (logs, metrics, costs)

## Value proposition

A lightweight, explainable CLI product that can become a scheduled intelligence workflow.
