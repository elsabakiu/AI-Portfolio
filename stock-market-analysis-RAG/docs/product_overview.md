# Product Overview

## One-line positioning

Stock Market RAG is a lightweight AI research copilot that answers strategic market questions using grounded evidence from SEC filings and earnings transcripts.

## Product value

- Reduces analyst/PM time-to-insight
- Improves confidence via source-grounded retrieval
- Creates repeatable research workflows instead of ad-hoc reading

## User outcomes

- Faster company comparison
- Better risk identification
- Clearer investment thesis articulation
